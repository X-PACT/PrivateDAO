## Usage Guidance

- Use the 1024 PNG for YouTube, Discord server icon source, and press kits.
- Use the 512 PNG for X, Telegram, and profile surfaces that downscale square avatars.
- Use the SVG when a platform or designer needs a lossless source.
- Keep the avatar on a dark background or let the asset render its own dark rounded-square field.
- Do not add extra text inside the avatar; keep `PrivateDAO` text outside when a platform also shows the account name.

## Design Intent

The mark combines:

- a Solana-inspired gradient ring
- a `delta` / triangle governance symbol
- a privacy lock
- a clean square avatar silhouette suited for platform cropping

It is designed to feel crypto-native, security-forward, and product-ready rather than playful or meme-led.
