# Encrypted Operations Lane

## What this is

The encrypted operations lane is a product surface that helps operators and reviewers shape a confidential treasury motion before it becomes a governed payout or settlement request.

It is not a claim of finished production secrecy by itself.

It is a structured planning surface for:

- operation profile
- privacy mode
- settlement posture
- operator visibility

## Why it exists

PrivateDAO already combines private governance, confidential payout posture, and settlement review surfaces.

This lane exists to make that system easier to use and easier to review by turning an encrypted treasury motion into one explicit operating plan.

That matters because confidential treasury work should not look like an opaque black box. It should stay understandable to the operator while preserving privacy where it matters.

## How it is used

1. choose the operation profile
2. choose the privacy mode
3. choose the settlement posture
4. choose the operator visibility level
5. copy or download the generated operation plan
6. carry the same plan into the security, services, and settlement review surfaces

## Current role in the product

This lane strengthens:

- confidential payout preparation
- reviewer-safe security explanation
- treasury operations continuity
- settlement packet follow-through

It should be treated as a structured operating surface that improves discipline around private treasury motion inside the live product.
