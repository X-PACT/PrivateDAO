# Enterprise DAO Pack

## Ideal user

An enterprise team, internal board, or operations group that needs confidential approvals for budgets, compensation, or strategic internal actions.

## Best PrivateDAO flow

1. Create an enterprise governance DAO.
2. Define approval and treasury roles.
3. Submit internal funding or compensation proposals.
4. Use private voting and optional confidential payout paths.
5. Finalize and execute under strict operator visibility.

## Why this pack is strong

- supports serious internal process without public intent leakage
- keeps operational discipline attached to treasury execution
- makes privacy, auditability, and operator evidence part of one system

## Technology fit

- **ZK:** recommended for stronger governance review boundaries
- **REFHE:** strongest fit when encrypted evaluation or compensation logic matters
- **MagicBlock:** useful when token-based confidential payments become part of enterprise treasury operations
- **Read node / RPC Fast path:** critical for enterprise-style operational visibility

## Pilot shape

- one internal board or finance group
- one approval template for budget or compensation
- one controlled treasury release path
- one operator and audit review packet per cycle
