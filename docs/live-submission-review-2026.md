## Current public build focus

The current cycle is pushing five product lanes upward together:

1. **Wallet-first governance**
   - reduce friction from DAO creation to proposal and vote flow
   - keep the proof path understandable for normal users and reviewers

2. **Confidential operations**
   - structure private treasury motion as a usable workflow
   - keep operator visibility and settlement posture explicit

3. **Telemetry and infrastructure**
   - make analytics, diagnostics, and export surfaces easier to read
   - strengthen the infrastructure story without overclaiming

4. **Treasury route and payments**
   - make governed swaps, rebalances, and payout funding easier to understand
   - keep route planning and execution context visible inside the product

5. **Audit-readiness and trust**
   - tighten hardening, trust, and runtime evidence into one coherent review path
