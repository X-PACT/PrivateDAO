# Strategic Opportunity Readiness 2026

This document keeps the non-primary opportunities around Frontier disciplined.

It is intentionally truth-aligned:

- it only points to surfaces that are already live
- it distinguishes shipped strength from the next real gap
- it avoids claiming partner integration, audit completion, or closed funding that does not exist

The point is not to collect names. The point is to keep PrivateDAO ready for the highest-value adjacent opportunities without drifting away from the core product thesis.
