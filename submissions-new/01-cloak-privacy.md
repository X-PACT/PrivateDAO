## Project description

PrivateDAO turns private governance and treasury execution into a wallet-first product flow that a normal operator can run in minutes: connect, review, sign, verify. Sensitive payout and treasury behavior is handled through privacy-ready settlement rails while proof continuity remains reviewer-visible.

## How this track is used

- Private settlement lane exists in product execution surfaces.
- Governance policy remains the control layer before any payout lane is used.
- Reviewer sees route-level trust path in `Judge` and `Proof`.
