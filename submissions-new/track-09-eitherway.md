## Short pitch

PrivateDAO integrates the Eitherway live dApp requirement as a production route: users connect wallet, sign a profile challenge, and continue to govern/execute/proof without leaving the main product shell.

## How we used the track

- Added dedicated route:
  - `/services/eitherway-live-dapp`
- Built wallet-first live component:
  - partner lane selector (Solflare, Kamino, DFlow, QuickNode)
  - use-case selector
  - sign-message profile challenge
  - local app profile persistence
  - operation receipt persistence via `operation_receipts`
- Wired judge and proof fast paths directly to Eitherway lane.
- Added Eitherway lane into technology map and services operational cards.
