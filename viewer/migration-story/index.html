Migration Story

PrivateDAO is not positioned as a greenfield-only DAO product.

The migration story is:

  • start with an existing governance or treasury process
  • move the voting surface into commit-reveal governance
  • move treasury execution into a deterministic DAO-controlled path
  • add confidential payout flows only where they are truly needed
  • preserve reviewer and operator visibility through runtime evidence

What can migrate first

  • treasury approval process
  • grant voting
  • internal budget approvals
  • contributor payment approvals

What can migrate later

  • encrypted payroll
  • bonus approvals
  • REFHE-bound evaluation paths
  • MagicBlock corridor settlement paths

Why the story works

This reduces migration risk because PrivateDAO does not force every organization to adopt every advanced feature at once.

The product can start as:

  • private governance + treasury control

and then grow into:

  • confidential organizational operations
PrivateDAO is a privacy-focused governance and treasury product on Solana with additive hardening, runtime evidence, and reviewer-ready proof surfaces.
